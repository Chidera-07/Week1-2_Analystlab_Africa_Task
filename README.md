# Week1-2_Analystlab_Africa_Task
The project focuses on transforming raw datasets into clean, analysis-ready data through comprehensive data cleaning, validation, and exploratory data analysis (EDA).

## 📂 Datasets
- **Online Retail Dataset** – Transactions, products, customers, and sales records.
- **Netflix Dataset** – Movies and TV shows metadata including ratings, genres, and countries.

## 🧹 Data Cleaning
The following cleaning tasks were performed:

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Standardized country names and text formatting
- Corrected data types and date formats
- Investigated negative quantities and prices
- Cleaned special characters and inconsistent descriptions


## 📈 Key Insights

### 🛒 Online Retail Dashboard

**KPIs**
- **$9.75M** Total Revenue
- **25.9K** Orders
- **4.4K** Customers
- **$17.99** Average Order Value
- **20%** Return Rate

**Insights**
- The **United Kingdom** generated over **$8.19M** in revenue, dominating all other markets.
- **WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER** was the most purchased product with **2,369 units sold**.
- **JUMBO BAG RED RETROSPOT** generated the highest revenue (**$47.36K**).
- Sales showed a steady increase from 2010 to 2011, peaking at **$1.54M in Q4 2011**.
- Customer purchases were highly concentrated, with one customer making over **135K purchases**.

---

### 🎬 Netflix Dashboard

**KPIs**
- **8.8K** Total Titles
- **6.1K** Movies
- **2.7K** TV Shows
- **TV-MA** was the most common rating

**Insights**
- Movies account for approximately **69%** of Netflix's catalog.
- The **United States** leads content production with **2,818 titles**, followed by **India (972)**.
- **TV-MA** and **TV-14** dominate the rating distribution, indicating a focus on mature audiences.
- The most common genre was **Dramas, International Movies**.
- Netflix experienced rapid content growth between **2016 and 2020**, reaching a peak of **1,879 additions**.
